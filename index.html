Strategy Deep Dive

AI First is a state of being, not an initiative. It is the goal — the cultural end-state where every employee, regardless of role, defaults to AI in their daily work. Enabled by AI is the parent initiative that houses all the actions, programmes, and workstreams driving the organisation toward that state. The training tracks are the "how." AI First is the "why" and the "where we're going."

📦 Use Case & Skills Library

A DevOps repository where employees browse, download, and contribute reusable AI assets. Contribute-back model — institutional AI knowledge compounds over time.

🔧
Skills
Downloadable MD files with prompt templates, agent configurations, and workflow automations. Like apps — find one, download, use.
💡
Use Cases
Documented examples of AI solving real Falck problems. Problem statement, approach, tool, result. Inspiration + proof points.
💬
Prompts
Tested, refined prompts for common tasks — writing summaries, analysing data, drafting communications. Copy and adapt.
